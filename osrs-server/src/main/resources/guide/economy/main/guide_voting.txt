Voting

You are able to vote either on the website or in-game by clicking on Vote Boosts, located under game information. By voting, you receive vote tickets, which can be exchanged with the Vote Manager located inside the Edgeville bank.

Your voting benefits last for 12 hours, and include the following:
<col=0000A0>*</col> 10% boosted drop rates (including all pets)
<col=0000A0>*</col> 10% more experience
<col=0000A0>*</col> 5% higher skilling success rates
